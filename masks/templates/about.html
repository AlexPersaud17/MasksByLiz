how it started

Peaches and Lemons face mask pop-up shop was a little side project I started this summer inspired by a sewing machine + loads of free time/something to do while I watched Schitts Creek + most importantly, keeping my loved ones safe. All masks are made with 100% cotton fabric and are washed prior to shipping for your safety. Hope you enjoy!
